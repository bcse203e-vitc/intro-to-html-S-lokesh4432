Ordered List: Steps to Make a Cup of Tea

  1. Boil water in a kettle.
  2. Place a tea bag in a cup.
  3. Pour the boiling water into the cup.
  4. Let it steep for 3-5 minutes.
  5. Remove the tea bag and enjoy.
